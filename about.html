About ClearSky

Transparent giving, powered by people who care.

ClearSky Foundation connects everyday donors with verified community campaigns — health, education, disaster relief, and more. Every contribution is tracked, every organizer is vetted, and 100% of your donation goes to the cause you support.

How ClearSky works

A simple path from cause to impact — for organizers and donors alike.

Organizers submit

Anyone can start a campaign in minutes using our 4-step wizard. Add your story, goal, and a cover image.

We verify

Every campaign is reviewed by our team within 24 hours to confirm the cause is legitimate and the organizer is real.

Donors give

Browse verified campaigns, pick a cause, and donate securely through Stripe. One-time or monthly — it's your choice.

Impact is tracked

Progress is visible on every campaign. Organizers post updates, donors see the results, and trust grows with every dollar.

Frequently asked

A few things donors and organizers ask most often.

How long does campaign review take?

Most campaigns are approved within 24 hours. Complex cases may take a little longer.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

It depends on the organizer's status. Check the campaign page or contact the organizer directly.

Can I cancel a monthly donation?

Yes. You'll receive a Stripe confirmation email — use the manage-subscription link inside it, or email us.

Privacy policy

Last updated April 2026

ClearSky Foundation ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information we collect

  • Campaign data you submit (title, story, images, organizer name, location).
  • Donation data processed by our payment provider (Stripe): name, email, amount, card details. We never store card details on our servers.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referrer — collected via browser-standard analytics and optional Meta / TikTok pixels.

How we use it

  • To operate the platform (display campaigns, route payments).
  • To verify campaign organizers and prevent fraud.
  • To send transactional email (donation receipts via Stripe).
  • To measure the effectiveness of our own advertising (aggregate only).

Your rights

You may request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@clearskyfoundation.org. Under GDPR and CCPA, we will respond within 30 days.

Third parties

We share data with: Stripe (payments), Supabase (hosting), and optionally Meta / TikTok (advertising pixels, where enabled). We never sell your data.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for the site to work and analytics cookies to understand usage. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser at any time.

Terms of use

Last updated April 2026

By using ClearSky Foundation, you agree to these terms.

Campaigns

  • Organizers must provide truthful information and a legitimate cause.
  • ClearSky reserves the right to approve, decline, or remove any campaign at its discretion.
  • Funds raised are transferred to the organizer via Stripe. ClearSky is not responsible for how organizers spend their funds, but we will investigate misuse reports.

Donations

  • All donations are final and non-refundable except in cases of confirmed fraud or duplicate charge.
  • Donations are processed by Stripe. Standard Stripe processing fees apply.
  • Monthly donations can be cancelled at any time via the Stripe receipt email.

Prohibited use

You may not use ClearSky to fund illegal activity, hateful content, political campaigns of office seekers, or personal expenses unrelated to the stated cause.

Liability

ClearSky provides the platform "as is". We are not liable for the actions of campaign organizers or the outcomes of funded campaigns. Our maximum liability is limited to the amount you donated in the previous 12 months.

Changes

We may update these terms from time to time. Material changes will be announced on this page with a new "last updated" date.
